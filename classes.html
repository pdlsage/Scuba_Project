    • Start your underwater adventure with the open water course. The goal of the class is to make you a comfortable and competent diver in the water. Our approach is to work to provide you a comfortable learning environment. This starts with a cost that includes everything for the course (no hidden fees) and using small classes to maximize your learning. The only thing that you need is your personal fit items, mask, fins and snorkel.

    This course lets you build on your basic diving skills while trying different disciplines or types of diving. If you find something you like then you can go on and learn more or use your new skills to practice more and get experience. Whether you are new diver or have a number of dives under your belt > this course allows you try new types of diving under supervision.

    In this course you will learn the basics of Underwater Digital Imaging. Mastering these skills will make you a better diver overall. Learn techniques to capture the best still and video shots underwater as well as digital processing and publishing techniques.
